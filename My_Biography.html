I was born in Somali in 1997 and I sooned moved to Kenya when I was barely 3 years old

I went to my primary school and the first two years of my highschool there

When I was in the second and third grade I used to do poorly in school and my "dugsi"

"Dugsi" is what someone will understand it as a sunday school but the difference is

that it was at least 5 days a week

One of my teachers in dugsi used to call me "crack head" and used to tell me that my head was filled with water

My teacher at school used to hate so much as I used to fail and do so poorly on every exam

And would sometimes punish me for no reason

So after two years of doing poorly in primary school my mom decided to repeat me in 3rd grade

And I felt like that was one of my best moments in my life

Now I had a new teacher (an older lady). She ignored my previous experience and

She started giving me private lessons so I could understand me

Belief it or not my situation changed
